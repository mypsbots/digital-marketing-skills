## Overview

Designs and nurtures a community (tribe) around shared values, stories and interests — where the
real value is members connecting with each other, not just with the brand. A strong community
champions the work and spreads it through authentic word of mouth.

## Business purpose

Community deepens belonging and retention and creates advocates; peer-to-peer connection and
genuine word of mouth are more trusted and durable than paid reach.

## When to use / when not to use

- **Use** to build an owned community, foster advocacy, or turn customers into champions.
- **Do not use** to astroturf, incentivise fake reviews, or extract from members without giving value.

## Inputs

### Required
- Audience, shared values/purpose, platform/space.

### Optional
- Existing members, content capacity, moderation resources.

## Outputs

- A **community blueprint** (purpose, values, structure, platform).
- An **engagement rituals plan** (recurring moments that build connection).
- An **advocacy and word-of-mouth plan** (how members champion the work).
- **Moderation guidelines** (safety, norms, inclusion).

## Methodology

1. Define the community's shared purpose and values ("people like us…").
2. Choose the space/platform where members already are or will gather.
3. Design member-to-member connection, not just broadcast; seed early belonging.
4. Establish rituals and rhythms (events, prompts, recognition) that sustain engagement.
5. Empower advocates and enable authentic word of mouth (ambassadors, referrals, UGC) — without buying fake endorsement.
6. Set moderation norms for safety, inclusion and trust.
7. Measure health (active members, connections, advocacy) and iterate generously.

## Decision rules

- Facilitate member-to-member value, not just brand-to-member.
- Consistency and generosity build trust; show up over the long term.
- Advocacy must be genuine; never fabricate or pay for fake reviews/endorsements.

## Quality checklist

- [ ] Clear shared purpose and values.
- [ ] Member-to-member connection designed, not just broadcast.
- [ ] Rituals sustain engagement; moderation protects trust.

## Compliance checklist

- [ ] Member data handled lawfully; privacy respected (PII present).
- [ ] Incentivised advocacy/referrals disclosed; no fake reviews (FTC/consumer rules).
- [ ] Moderation meets platform and safety obligations.

## Failure conditions and escalation

- Missing purpose/platform → `insufficient_data`.
- Safety, harassment or data incidents → escalate to trust & safety / legal.

## Examples

- **SaaS:** user community sharing workflows; power users as advocates.
- **E-commerce:** brand community/subculture around shared identity and UGC.
- **Real estate:** local community/network built on genuine relationships.
- **Retail:** loyalty community with events and member recognition.

## KPIs and metrics

Active members, member-to-member interactions, retention of members, referral/word-of-mouth,
advocacy actions, and sentiment.
