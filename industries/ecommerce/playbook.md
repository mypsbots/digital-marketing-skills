# E-commerce Marketing Playbook

> An end-to-end playbook for growing an online store: acquire efficiently, lift AOV,
> retain customers and protect margin. Always reason in contribution margin, not just revenue.

## When to use this playbook

Use for D2C/B2C (and some B2B) stores selling physical or digital goods online, single- or
multi-channel.

## North-star and economics

- Prefer a **contribution-margin** north-star over gross revenue.
- Track **ROAS, MER (blended), AOV, conversion rate, repeat purchase rate, LTV, CAC, margin**.
- Guardrails: protect blended MER and per-order contribution margin; watch discount depth.

## Growth levers

1. **Acquisition efficiency** — `paid-social.catalogue-campaign-planning`,
   `paid-search.campaign-architecture`, `seo.topic-cluster-planning`.
2. **Conversion** — `ecommerce.conversion-rate-improvement`,
   `cro.landing-page-audit`, `experimentation.ab-test-design`.
3. **AOV** — bundling, thresholds, cross-sell (offer design).
4. **Retention & LTV** — `ecommerce.repeat-purchase`, `crm.lifecycle-stage-design`,
   `email.lead-nurture-series`.
5. **Promotions** — margin-aware seasonal planning (`retail.seasonal-retail-campaign`).

## Funnel stages and priority skills

- **Foundation/Research** — `customer-research.buyer-persona-development`,
  `market-research.competitor-positioning-analysis`.
- **Strategy** — `strategy.full-funnel-strategy`, `budgeting.channel-budget-allocation`.
- **Measurement** — `analytics.measurement-strategy`, `analytics.utm-governance`,
  `reporting.monthly-marketing-report`.

## Recommended workflows

- `marketing-strategy-development`
- `meta-ads-launch` (catalogue/dynamic ads; approval-gated)
- `google-ads-launch` (shopping/search; approval-gated)
- `seo-content-cluster`
- `email-nurture-launch` (consent + approval gated)

## Compliance and safety notes

- Honest pricing, urgency and scarcity — no fabricated countdowns or fake stock.
- Genuine reviews only; transparent fees/shipping surfaced early.
- Email/SMS require consent; spend/sends are approval-gated.

## KPIs to report

Contribution margin, ROAS, MER, AOV, CVR, repeat rate, LTV, CAC, sell-through.
