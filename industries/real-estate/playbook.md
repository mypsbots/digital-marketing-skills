# Real Estate Marketing Playbook

> An end-to-end playbook for compliant real-estate lead generation, qualification and
> agent follow-up. Speed-to-lead and fair, accurate advertising are decisive.

## When to use this playbook

Use for agents, brokerages, developers and property services marketing to buyers, sellers,
landlords or tenants.

## North-star and economics

- North-star: **qualified enquiries** (or completions/instructions for longer cycles).
- Track **CPL, cost per qualified lead (CPQL), lead-to-viewing, viewing-to-offer,
  cost per completion, speed-to-lead**.

## The lead engine

1. **Generate** — `real-estate.lead-generation-campaign` across local paid search,
   paid social and portals; accurate listings only.
2. **Capture** — fast landing pages + instant routing (`cro.landing-page-audit`).
3. **Qualify** — `real-estate.lead-qualification` (budget, timeline, financing, intent).
4. **Follow up** — `real-estate.agent-follow-up-workflow` with rapid, multi-touch cadence.
5. **Nurture** — `email.lead-nurture-series` and `crm.workflow-design` (consent-based).

## Funnel stages and priority skills

- **Foundation/Research** — `customer-research.buyer-persona-development`,
  `market-research.competitor-positioning-analysis`.
- **Strategy/Budget** — `strategy.channel-mix-planning`,
  `budgeting.channel-budget-allocation`.
- **Measurement** — `analytics.measurement-strategy`, `reporting.campaign-performance-report`.

## Recommended workflows

- `real_estate_lead_plan` (prompt) and `meta-ads-launch` / `google-ads-launch`
- `email-nurture-launch` (consent + approval gated)
- `monthly-reporting`

## Compliance and safety notes

- **Fair-housing/fair-advertising**: never target or exclude on protected characteristics
  where prohibited; avoid proxies. Screen with `compliance.marketing-compliance-assessment`.
- **Accuracy**: listing details, prices and availability must be truthful and current.
- Automated SMS/email require consent; follow-up automation is approval-gated.

## KPIs to report

Qualified enquiries, CPL, CPQL, speed-to-lead, lead-to-viewing, viewing-to-offer.
