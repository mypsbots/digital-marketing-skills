# SaaS Marketing Playbook

> An end-to-end playbook for marketing a SaaS product across acquisition, activation,
> retention and expansion. Ground every plan in unit economics and privacy by design.

## When to use this playbook

Use for B2B or B2C software sold on subscription/usage pricing, whether product-led (PLG),
sales-led (SLG) or hybrid.

## North-star and economics

- Choose a **north-star metric** reflecting delivered value (e.g. weekly active accounts,
  activated workspaces), not vanity signups.
- Track unit economics: **CAC, CAC payback, LTV, LTV:CAC, NRR, gross/net churn**.
- Guardrails: CAC payback within target months; NRR ≥ 100% for healthy expansion.

## Motion selection

| Motion | Fits when | Primary funnel |
| --- | --- | --- |
| PLG | Low ACV, fast time-to-value, self-serve | Signup → activation → upgrade → expansion |
| SLG | High ACV, complex buying, multiple stakeholders | MQL → SQL → opportunity → close |
| Hybrid | Bottom-up adoption + enterprise expansion | PLG base + sales-assist for expansion |

- `seo-content-cluster`
- `google-ads-launch` / `meta-ads-launch` (approval-gated)
- `email-nurture-launch` (consent + approval gated)

## Compliance and safety notes

- Cold outreach lawful basis varies by region — screen with `compliance.consent-assessment`.
- No guaranteed-growth claims. Present forecasts as ranges with assumptions.
- Any spend or send is gated by human approval.

## KPIs to report

North-star, signups, activation rate, CAC, payback, NRR, LTV:CAC, pipeline coverage.
