# Retail Marketing Playbook

> An end-to-end playbook for omnichannel retail: drive footfall and online sales,
> run margin-aware promotions and build loyalty. Measure incrementality, protect stock and margin.

## When to use this playbook

Use for physical stores and omnichannel retailers (including BOPIS/click-and-collect) marketing
locally and nationally.

## North-star and economics

- North-star: **omnichannel revenue** (or contribution margin) with footfall as an input.
- Track **footfall, incremental sales, redemption rate, basket/AOV, margin, loyalty active rate,
  ROAS/MER**.

## Core motions

1. **Local demand** — `retail.local-promotion` (local search/social, GBP, email/SMS, in-store).
2. **Seasonal peaks** — `retail.seasonal-retail-campaign` with phased calendar and stock safeguards.
3. **Loyalty & retention** — `retail.loyalty-programme`, `ecommerce.repeat-purchase`,
   `crm.lifecycle-stage-design`.
4. **Conversion (online)** — `ecommerce.conversion-rate-improvement`.

## Funnel stages and priority skills

- **Foundation/Research** — `customer-research.buyer-persona-development`,
  `market-research.competitor-positioning-analysis`.
- **Strategy/Budget** — `strategy.channel-mix-planning`,
  `budgeting.channel-budget-allocation`, `budgeting.scenario-planning`.
- **Measurement** — `analytics.measurement-strategy`, `reporting.monthly-marketing-report`.

## Recommended workflows

- `retail_promotion_plan` (prompt)
- `meta-ads-launch` / `google-ads-launch` (local; approval-gated)
- `email-nurture-launch` (consent + approval gated)
- `monthly-reporting`

## Compliance and safety notes

- Promotion terms must be transparent and honest; honour advertised stock/prices.
- Ensure inventory supports promoted demand; cap offers when stock is thin.
- Email/SMS and loyalty comms require consent; spend/sends are approval-gated.

## KPIs to report

Omnichannel revenue, footfall, incremental sales, redemption, basket/AOV, margin, loyalty activity.
