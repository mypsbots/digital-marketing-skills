# Roadmap

This roadmap is indicative and will evolve. Nothing here is a commitment or a guarantee.

## Now

- Complete, deeply-detailed skills across all 32 categories.
- Working MCP server (stdio + HTTP) exposing skills as resources, curated prompts and a set of
  read-only tools plus dry-run write tools.
- Central metrics catalogue with formulas, interpretation and limitations.
- Mock connectors for analytics, advertising, social, CRM, email and commerce.
- Approval engine, audit engine and policy engine.
- Python utilities for statistics, forecasting and content-quality evaluation.

## Next

- Expand every named skill in the master catalogue to full depth with industry examples.
- Live connectors for major platforms (read-only first), gated behind feature flags and
  credentials, with contract tests.
- Additional workflows and a workflow execution UI-friendly output format.
- Expanded LLM evaluation suite and regression harness.
- MkDocs documentation site with published guides.

## Later

- Additional industries beyond SaaS, e-commerce, real estate and retail.
- Optional multi-tenant deployment guidance and observability dashboards.
- Community skill marketplace conventions.

## Non-goals

- Guaranteeing marketing outcomes.
- Performing paid or external actions without explicit human approval.
- Replacing qualified legal, financial, tax or regulatory advice.
